Frequently Asked Questions

How does text marketing work?

Send personalized SMS campaigns directly from RetainPro. Target customers based on purchase history, location, or preferences. Track delivery, opens, and responses in real-time. No additional apps required for customers.

Can I personalize offers for different customer groups?

Absolutely. Segment customers by purchase history, spending level, location, or custom tags. Send VIP offers to top customers, win-back campaigns to inactive ones, and targeted promotions based on past purchases.

Will surveys help me improve customer satisfaction?

Yes! Automated post-purchase and service surveys capture real-time feedback. Identify issues before they become problems, spot improvement opportunities, and show customers their opinions matter. Happy customers stay longer.

Is my customer data secure?

Customer data is encrypted and stored on secure, SOC 2 compliant servers. We never share or sell customer information. You own your data and can export it anytime. Bank-level security protects every interaction.

Do customers need to download an app?

No downloads required. Customers receive text messages on any phone. Surveys and offers work through simple web links. Zero friction means higher engagement and response rates.

How do I measure repeat business results?

Built-in analytics track customer lifetime value, repeat purchase rates, campaign performance, and retention metrics. See exactly which campaigns drive results and which customers are most valuable to your business.

Can I automate follow-up campaigns?

Yes! Set up automated sequences for post-purchase follow-ups, reorder reminders, birthday offers, and win-back campaigns. Customers receive personalized messages at optimal times without any manual work.

What if customers opt out of text messages?

Customers can opt out anytime with a simple text reply. We handle all compliance automatically. Focus on providing value, and opt-out rates stay low while engagement stays high.

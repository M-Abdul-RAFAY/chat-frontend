Yes. Hi Chat integrates directly with your website to add live chat functionality and capture all contact form submissions. Every website interaction flows into your unified inbox for immediate follow-up and conversion.

Absolutely. Hi Chat connects with Facebook Messenger, Instagram DMs, and WhatsApp to centralize all social media conversations. You can respond to social inquiries alongside phone calls and texts from one dashboard.

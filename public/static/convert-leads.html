Frequently Asked Questions

How do text-to-pay and digital invoices work?

Simply send a secure payment link via SMS or email. Customers click once to pay—no app downloads or account creation needed. Payment is processed instantly through Stripe or your preferred gateway.

Can I automate payment reminders?

Yes! Set up automated sequences for overdue invoices, upcoming appointments, or follow-up payments. Choose timing, messaging, and channels (SMS, email, or both).

Does it work with my CRM or scheduling software?

ConvertPro integrates with 500+ tools including HubSpot, Salesforce, Calendly, and QuickBooks. Lead data syncs automatically—no manual entry required.

How fast can I respond to leads with the mobile app?

Instantly. Get push notifications for new leads and respond within seconds. The mobile app includes full chat functionality, payment collection, and lead management.

Is it secure to send payment links by SMS?

Absolutely. All payment links are encrypted and PCI-compliant. Customers are directed to secure payment pages, and all transactions are protected by bank-level security.

How much does it cost?

Plans start at $49/month for small businesses. Includes unlimited text-to-pay, automation, and mobile access. Enterprise plans available for larger teams. All plans include a 14-day free trial.

What if my leads prefer phone calls over text?

ConvertPro supports all communication channels. Take calls, send follow-up texts, emails, and payment links—all from one platform. Use whatever method your customers prefer.

Can I customize the automated messages?

Yes! All automated messages are fully customizable. Add your branding, personalize with customer data, and adjust timing to match your business style.
